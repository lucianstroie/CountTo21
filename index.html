Welcome to CountTo21


This is a card counting game that uses probability to inform betting decisions.


How to play:

- Your cards are in the center.

- Place smaller bets during the first few rounds.

- Increase bet size if the probability of getting a 17 or higher is above that it was during round 0. The larger the difference, the bigger the increase in bet size.

- The decision to hit or stay is informed by the expected value of the cards still in the deck.

- The AI will make betting decisions based on the dealer’s top card, weighing the risk of hitting to the dealer’s expected hand calculated from their top card.


Notes:

- There are two decks, so there typically will be 9-10 rounds.

- The two players to your left and right always follow the AI recommendation and were included to show the change in the probability distribution function sooner.
